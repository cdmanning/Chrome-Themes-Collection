Overview

Starswept Development ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Chrome Theme Collection at themes.starswept.dev. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or physical address unless you voluntarily provide it by contacting us directly.

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how users interact with our theme gallery. This includes information such as:

  • General geographic location (Region/Country level only).
  • Device type and browser information.

Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies to identify unique visitors. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings or use browser extensions to opt-out of Google Analytics tracking.
